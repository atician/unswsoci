Weekly Games

The Social Soccer Society runs regular biweekly games on Wednesday and Friday from 2.30 - 4.30 from Weeks 1 - 13 during Semester. These games are run casually with no set positions or official rules, and players are feel to come and go as they please. After these casual games, we head to the Roundhouse to continue socialising.
