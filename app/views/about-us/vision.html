Vision

The initial vision of the society when it was first founded on Monday 21st March 2011 was the following:

  1. To facilitate an organisational structure & easier networking of social soccer games at UNSW
  2. To help soccer playing enthusiasts connected to each other
  3. To provide cheap/free casual football games for members
  4. To provide funding for proper equipment to improve casual soccer playing experience
  5. To provide funding for proper organised events.
As subsequent years went by, this vision has changed and adapted to the environment and needs of current members
